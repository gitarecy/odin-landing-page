Protective Styles

Natural hair protective styles are a popular choice among individuals with textured hair seeking to promote healthy hair growth and minimize damage. From braids and twists to updos and buns, these styles help safeguard the hair by reducing manipulation and exposure to external factors, while showcasing the beauty and versatility of natural hair.

Pick your own style to protect those beautiful kinky curls and coils!

Braids

Knotless box braids are a modern and comfortable alternative to traditional box braids. They eliminate tight knots at the roots, reducing scalp tension and offering a more natural and versatile protective hairstyle for textured hair.

Locs

Locs, also known as dreadlocks, are a popular hairstyle for textured hair where strands of hair are intentionally matted and twisted over time. They offer a unique and expressive look, showcasing the beauty and cultural significance of natural hair.

Cornrows

Cornrows are a stylish and practical protective hairstyle where hair is tightly braided close to the scalp, providing a sleek and low-maintenance option for textured hair.
